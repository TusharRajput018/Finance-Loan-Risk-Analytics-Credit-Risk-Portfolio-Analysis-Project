# Assumptions and Limitations

## Assumptions
- All provided data is accurate and collected from reliable internal systems
- Missing values have been handled using standard industry practices
- Credit score bands accurately represent borrower risk
- Economic conditions are assumed to be stable during the analysis period

## Limitations
- The dataset does not include real default or recovery outcomes
- External economic factors are not considered
- Behavioral changes after loan approval are not captured
- Regional classifications are broad and may hide local variations

These limitations should be considered when interpreting the results.
