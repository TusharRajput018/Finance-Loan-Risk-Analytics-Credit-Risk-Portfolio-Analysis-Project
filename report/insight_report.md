# Insights Report

## Credit Risk Trends
Borrowers with lower credit scores consistently fall into higher risk buckets. Credit score bands provide a clear separation of risk levels.

## Loan Structure Analysis
Loans with high loan-to-value ratios show increased exposure and lower interest income stability.

## Income & DTI Impact
High debt-to-income ratios significantly increase the likelihood of elevated risk classification.

## Regional Insights
Certain regions contribute disproportionately to total exposure, suggesting geographic concentration risk.

## Business Value
The analysis supports proactive risk monitoring and helps decision-makers refine loan approval and pricing strategies.
